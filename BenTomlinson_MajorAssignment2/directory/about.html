Our Story

We are Conestoga students who love to play and teach guitar. From hard rock to folk, we aim to provide an affordable, personally tailored guitar teaching experience. As college students, we understand that most guitar lessons are too expensive for a student budget. 31 Amherst Guitar & Bass lessons are the soloution to this problem.
